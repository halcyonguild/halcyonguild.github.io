Halcyon was formed early 2013 so that a group of friends could game together.

Our goal has always remained the same; efficient raiding WITHOUT enforcing alts, PTR tests, split runs or a treadmill grind inbetween tiers. Alts are welcomed, but not enforced. Above all is maintaining a chill and enjoyable raid environment.

As the clear time of tiers shortened, we decided, in Battle for Azeroth, that our old 9 hour schedule did not make sense anymore for our enjoyment of the game. As such in the first week of mythic progress we raid the first 5 days starting 18:00. Immediately after this initial week we go back to our regular progress hours:

Progress Raiding hours after first mythic week:

  • Wed - 20.00-23.00 ST
  • Thur - 20.00-23.00 ST
  • Sun - 18.00-23.00 ST (usually weekly clear)

We never deviate from our planned schedule. A rigid schedule is important to us such that we may plan real life around it.
