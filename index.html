Halcyon was formed early 2013 out of the need for a place for friends to raid together.

Our goal has always remained the same; efficient raiding without enforcing alts, PTR tests, split runs or a treadmill grind inbetween tiers. Above all is maintaining a healthy and enjoyable raid environment.

To fully enjoy early mythic progress, in Battle for Azeroth we decided to raid the first 5 days of a new tier starting 18:00. Immediately after this initial week we go back to our regular progress hours:

Progress Raiding hours after first mythic week:

  • Wed - 20.00-23.00 ST
  • Thur - 20.00-23.00 ST
  • Sun - 18.00-23.00 ST (usually weekly clear)

We never deviate from our planned schedule by adding surprise raid days or extending raids. A rigid schedule is important to us such that we may plan real life around it. We don't require alts from our raiders, you are welcome to, but never required. Our logs are always publicly available on our WarcraftLogs page.
